# Funnel Copy Skill

Comprehensive copywriting reference for every funnel element.

## Headline Formulas

### Benefit-Driven
1. How to [Desired Result] Without [Pain/Effort]
2. The [Number]-Step System to [Result] in [Timeframe]
3. [Number] [Audience Members] Are Already [Achieving Result]. Here's How to Join Them
4. What [Authority Figures] Know About [Topic] That You Don't
5. Stop [Frustrating Activity]. Start [Desired Activity] Instead
6. The Secret to [Result] That [Experts/Competitors] Don't Want You to Know

### Curiosity-Driven
1. This [Small Change] Helped [Person/Company] [Achieve X Result]
2. Why [Conventional Wisdom] Is Dead Wrong (And What to Do Instead)
3. The [Unexpected Thing] That [Desirable Outcome]
4. [Surprising Statistic] — And What It Means for Your [Area]

### Urgency-Driven
1. [X] Days Left to [Get Benefit / Avoid Loss]
2. Last Chance: [Offer] Closes [Date]
3. Only [X] Spots Left for [Event/Offer]

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objection
"I understand the investment feels significant. Consider this: [product] pays for itself when you [specific ROI scenario]. Our customers typically see [X result] within [timeframe]. Plus, with our [guarantee], there's zero risk."

### Time Objection
"I get it — you're busy. That's exactly why we built [product] to take only [X minutes/hours]. Most of our customers set it up in [timeframe] and save [X hours] every [week/month] after that."

### Trust Objection
"You might be wondering if this really works. Here's what [Customer Name] said after [timeframe]: '[specific testimonial quote]'. We've helped [number] [people/companies] achieve [result], and we back everything with a [guarantee]."

### "I need to think about it"
"Totally fair. Here's what I'd suggest: [summarize the key benefit in one sentence]. The only thing that changes by waiting is [specific urgency — price increase, spots filling, etc.]. Either way, you're covered by our [guarantee]."
